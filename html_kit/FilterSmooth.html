Apply a smoothening filter kernel to images.

This augmenter has identical outputs to calling PIL.Image.filter with kernel PIL.ImageFilter.SMOOTH.

Added in 0.4.0.

Supported dtypes:

See filter_smooth().

seedNone or int or imgaug.random.RNG or numpy.random.Generator or numpy.random.BitGenerator or numpy.random.SeedSequence or numpy.random.RandomState, optional

See __init__().

See __init__().
